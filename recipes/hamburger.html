Hamburguer

Description:

Hamburger, maybe the most popular dish around the world (weeeell... maybe not as popular as pizza), and now I will teach you how to make it!!

Firt, lest begin with the ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams ground beef
  • 250 grams ground pork
  • 150 grams bacon, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1/2 cups breadcrumbs
  • parsley, chopped finely, to taste
  • salt, and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Mix the beef, pork and bacon in a bowl.
  2. Add the whole egg and mustard, mix well.
  3. Season and add the chopped parsley.
  4. Add the bread crumbs and integrate all the ingredients perfectly.
  5. Take a portion of meat and form the hamburger of the desired size.
  6. Cook in a pan or on a spit with a little oil until well cooked.
